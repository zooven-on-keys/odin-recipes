Stir Fried Ginger Broccoli Beef

Tender beef slices and crunchy Chinese broccoli or kale are stir-fried in a savory ginger sauce. Easy to make, pretty healthy, and everybody loves it!

Ingredients

  • 7 ounces beef sirloin, sliced into strips
  • 1 1/4 tsps teaspoons chicken bouillon granules, divided
  • 1 tsp white sugar, divided
  • 1 tbsp ginger juice
  • 3 tbsps water, divided
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsps vegetable oil, divided
  • 1 (4 inch) piece ginger root, peeled and sliced
  • 14 ounces Chinese broccoli, sliced

Steps

  1. Toss beef strips with 1 teaspoon chicken bouillon granules and 3/4 teaspoon sugar in a bowl. Marinate in the refrigerator, 20 minutes to 1 hour.
  2. Combine remaining 1/4 teaspoon chicken bouillon granules, 1/4 teaspoon sugar, and ginger juice in a bowl.
  3. Mix 2 tablespoons water with cornstarch in a small bowl. Add to bowl with ginger juice mixture; stir.
  4. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a wok over medium-high heat; cook and stir ginger until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Add broccoli; cook and stir for 2 minutes. Add remaining 1 tablespoon water, cover, and cook until broccoli is cooked but still crunchy, about 1 minute. Transfer to a dish.
  5. Heat remaining 1 tablespoon oil in the wok. Add beef; cook and stir to medium well doneness, about 4 minutes. Add ginger juice mixture and broccoli. Cook and stir until heated through, about 2 minutes.
